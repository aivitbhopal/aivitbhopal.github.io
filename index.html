AI Conclave 2.0

18th-20th November, 2022

SEARCH | PLAN | ENGAGE | CREATE

AI Conclave is a 2-day international event, held on 26th and 27th February 2021. It aims to impart knowledge and raise awareness about Artificial Intelligence and related technologies in the mind of the enthusiastic youth of India. AI Conclave 2021 is a medium through which you may carve your path to learn more about AI and ML. Loaded with discussions, quizzes, keynotes sessions by subject matter experts, AI Conclave helps students gain sufficient knowledge and enhance their competence and expertise. Speakers from around the globe are invited to share their knowledge through Sessions on Deep Learning, Neural Networks, Data Science, etc
