AI Conclave AI Conclave AI Conclave

18th-21st November, 2022

LEARN | BUILD | INNOVATE

AI Conclave is a 4-day international event, held from 18th to 21st November 2022. It aims to impart knowledge and raise awareness about Artificial Intelligence and Industry 4.0 amongst the enthusiastic youth of India. Loaded with ideations, hackathons, and keynotes sessions by domain experts, AI Conclave helps students gain sufficient knowledge and enhance their competence and expertise. Speakers from around the globe are invited to share their knowledge through Sessions on Deep Learning, Neural Networks, Data Science, etc. Acknowledging students about the innumerable technologies that incessantly evolve through AI and furnish remarkable opportunities for learning and practicing, is AI Conclave’s primary objective.
