Welcome to AI Conclave 2024, A 3-day thrill ride from February 5th to 7th, hosted by the AI Club at VIT Bhopal University. Immerse yourself in industry insights through ideations, hackathons, and expert keynotes. Explore NLP, MLOps, Computer Vision, etc. Experience hands-on workshops, expert dialogues, and tech exhibits from 10 am to 6 pm. Save the dates!

OUR SPONSOR

CPF ( CyberPeace Foundation ) is a India headquartered award-winning civil society organisation and think tank of cybersecurity and policy experts, having a global footprint. It has been setup with the core objective to work against Cyber Warfare, Cyber Weapons, Cyber Crime and Cyber Terrorism. CPF is involved in Policy Advocacy, Research and Training related to Global Cyber Peace, Cyber Security and Privacy Issues. Key areas of our work are in Technology Governance, Policy Review and Advocacy, Capacity and Capability creation and building through partnerships with various government organizations, academic institutions and civil society entities.
