About Us

VIT Bhopal University has a goal with a new global outlook that empowers its aspirants to attain excellence through learning. The objective is to educate students from all over India, and from other countries, for them to become enlightened individuals, improving the standards of the society.

Here at VIT Bhopal’s AI Division, we give credence to practical skills and hands-on experience to develop and shape the career orientation of students.

AI Club is the newly launched technical club of VIT Bhopal. Where we promise to deliver far-ranging knowledge about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to students. The purpose of this club is to give students an insight into what is needed to make their work persistent in the industrial world.

AI Conclave

AI Conclave is a 2-day Nationwide event, held on 26th and 27th February 2021. It aims to impart knowledge and raise awareness about Artificial Intelligence and related technologies in the mind of the enthusiastic youth of India. AI Conclave 2021 is a medium through which you may carve your path to learn more about AI and ML. Loaded with discussions, quizzes, keynotes sessions by subject matter experts, AI Conclave helps students gain sufficient knowledge and enhance their competence and expertise. Speakers from around the globe are invited to share their knowledge through Sessions on Deep Learning, Neural Networks, Data Science, etc. Acknowledging students about the innumerable technologies that incessantly evolve through AI and furnish remarkable opportunities in learning and practicing, is AI Conclave’s primary objective.

Keynote Speakers

Dr. Xavier Fernando
Professor, Ryerson University, Canada

Dr. Xavier Fernando

Dr. Fernando is one of the most revered professors at the Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Ryerson University, Toronto, Canada, with an experience of almost 20 years at the University.

He is bestowed with honours and awards worldwide. One of them being a top 25 Immigrants Award by Ryerson University. Moreover, he has authored and Co-authored over 200 research papers, along with 3 patents and 3 books.

Being a dignified member of IEEE, he has delivered over 50 Sessions globally and he has also held many leadership positions including Chairing IEEE Toronto Section, Organizing Canadian Conference on Electrical and Computer Engineering etc during his time.

Dr. Onkar Krishna
Research Associate, NTT Corporation, Japan

Dr. Onkar Krishna

Dr. Onkar Krishna is the gem of our conclave. At the age of 32, he has won numerous awards and scholarships. Currently he is based in Japan where he completed his PhD.

He began his research in 2011 and since then he has been featured in international publications.

With a dashing personality he is also an excellent speaker. He has been invited to 10+ IEEE conferences and has given presentations at UC Berkeley, CNRS, MIT, NTT Tokyo and many reputed institutes.

His research interests include Computer vision, cloud processing, pattern recognition and the list goes on. He has 2 patents in the same field as well.

Dr. Shailesh Kumar
Chief Data Scientist, Centre of Excellence in AIML, Jio

Dr. Shailesh Kumar

Dr. Shailesh Kumar has over 20 years of experience in the AI Innovation field. Currently he is Chief Data Scientist at Jio, and his previous experiences include companies like Google, Microsoft, Yahoo, Ola, FICO, Third Leap and many more esteemed Tech Giants.

Being invited to sessions by TEDx, NASSCOM, UNESCO, Analytics Vidhya, Times Group, World Economic forum and additional conferences, he is an exceptional speaker and his guidance is impeccable.

He also engages himself in educating the next generation of AI Engineers. He is a visiting faculty member at IIIT Hyderabad and Indian School of Business.

Mr. Rudradeb Mitra
CEO and Founder, Omdena, USA

Mr. Rudradeb Mitra

Rudradeb has completed his MPhil from University of Cambridge. He has vast experience in AI, he is an AI Engineer turned Entrepreneur. With over 6 startups, 10 research papers, he is also an exceptional speaker.

Moreover, he has also penned a book on AI and his articles on Medium have amazing reviews as well. He was previously a product lead mentor for Google for Startups, Senior advisor for AI at Efma, Lead Mentor at ReaktorX.

Currently he is the CEO and founder of Omdena. Omdena is a collaborative platform to build AI solutions for real word problems.

Dr Lalit Garg
Senior Lecturer, University of Malta

Dr Lalit Garg

Lalit Garg is an Information Technology innovator, academician, learner, teacher, developer, keynote speaker, Consultant, and researcher with a demonstrated history of working in the higher education industry and an excellent research publication record. He is skilled in solving complex problems using machine learning, and data analytics, especially from the medicine and healthcare domain. Presently he is a Senior Lecturer in Computer Information Systems at the University of Malta, Msida, Malta. He is also an honorary lecturer at the University of Liverpool, UK.
