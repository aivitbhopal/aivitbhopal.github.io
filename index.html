AI Conclave 2.0

18th-21th November, 2022

LEARN | BUILD | INNOVATE

Al Conclave 2022 is a 4-day international event from 18th to 21st November 2022. It aims to impart knowledge and raise awareness about the contribution of Artificial Intelligence in Industry 4.0 in the mind of enthusiastic youth all around the globe. Loaded with Competitions and Sessions by subject matter experts, Al Conclave helps students gain sufficient knowledge and enhance their competence and expertise. Expert Speakers from a variety of industries ranging from Agritech to Edtech have been invited to share with students the knowledge on how Al is leveraging their respective industries.
