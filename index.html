About Us

ABOUT US

"Success in creating effective AI could be the biggest event in the history of our civilization. Or the worst. We just don't know." -Stephen Hawking

AI4U is a nationwide weekend-long workshop organized by VIT Bhopal, to guide young minds to ride along with the technology of the future world. In this skill development initiative by the university, students would be given insights and introduced to limitless future possibilities of AI along with engaging them in a first-hand experience of building real-time AI applications under the guidance of AI-Specialised students and faculty members. The AI4U Workshop brings together over 2,000 representatives countrywide to engage in meaningful conversations and gives a unique exposure to every individual about the AI-powered real-world possibilities.

About the Ideathon

A short brainstorming session to bring to light the fresh solutions that the next generation possesses, to solve certain challenges in a new and innovative way. Working as an individual or a group using the concept and application of Artificial Intelligence to reach at possible solutions. Transforming the way of thinking and providing a competitive environment for the young talents.
