About Us

VIT Bhopal University has a goal with a new global outlook that empowers its aspirants to attain excellence through learning. The objective is to educate students from all over India, and from other countries, for them to become enlightened individuals, improving the standards of the society.

Here at VIT Bhopal’s AI Division, we give credence to practical skills and hands-on experience to develop and shape the career orientation of students.

AI Club is the newly launched technical club of VIT Bhopal. Where we promise to deliver far-ranging knowledge about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to students. The purpose of this club is to give students an insight into what is needed to make their work persistent in the industrial world.

AI Conclave

AI Conclave is a 2-day Nationwide event, held on 26th and 27th February 2021. It aims to impart knowledge and raise awareness about Artificial Intelligence and related technologies in the mind of the enthusiastic youth of India. AI Conclave 2021 is a medium through which you may carve your path to learn more about AI and ML. Loaded with discussions, quizzes, keynotes sessions by subject matter experts, AI Conclave helps students gain sufficient knowledge and enhance their competence and expertise. Speakers from around the globe are invited to share their knowledge through Sessions on Deep Learning, Neural Networks, Data Science, etc. Acknowledging students about the innumerable technologies that incessantly evolve through AI and furnish remarkable opportunities in learning and practicing, is AI Conclave’s primary objective.
